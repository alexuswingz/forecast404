Settings Guide

Forecast Multiplier

Key setting for matching Excel values. Adjusts the base forecast to calibrate with your Excel spreadsheet. Higher values = more aggressive forecast = lower DOI, higher Units to Make. Default: 3.1

Amazon DOI Goal

Target days of inventory coverage for Amazon. Default: 120 days

Inbound Lead Time

Days to ship inventory to Amazon (shipping time). Default: 30 days

Manufacture Lead Time

Days to produce inventory (production time). Default: 7 days

Market Adjustment

Year-over-year growth assumption applied to 18+ month forecasts. Set to 0.05 for 5% expected growth. Default: 0.05

Velocity Adjustment

Controls how much recent sales velocity affects forecasts. Default: 24.0

Velocity Weight

Weight applied to velocity adjustment. Higher = more emphasis on recent trends. Default: 0.15 (15%)

Timezone Offset

GMT offset for date calculations to match your Excel. Default: -7 (Pacific Time, GMT-7)
